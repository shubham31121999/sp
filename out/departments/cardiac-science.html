Comprehensive Cardiology Services

Full spectrum of Heart care

Our cardiac services support every stage of heart health, from early detection and prevention to critical care and recovery, providing complete, patient-focused care.

Coronary Care Unit (CCU)

A dedicated intensive care unit for critically ill cardiac patients. Provides advanced monitoring and treatment for heart attacks, arrhythmias, and acute coronary syndromes using continuous cardiac telemetry and life-support systems.

24x7 Chest Pain Clinic

An emergency service available at all times for the rapid evaluation and management of chest pain. Equipped with ECG, cardiac enzyme testing, and imaging for quick diagnosis of heart attacks and other cardiovascular conditions.

Executive Cardiac Checkups:

Comprehensive screening designed for executives and individuals at increased risk of heart disease. Includes ECG, echocardiography, lipid profile, treadmill testing (TMT), and expert consultations to detect early cardiac issues.

Preventive Cardiology

Focused on reducing cardiovascular risk through regular screenings, lifestyle counseling, and medication management. Addresses conditions such as hypertension, diabetes, obesity, and high cholesterol.

Structured Cardiac Rehabilitation

A medically supervised program for patients recovering from heart attacks or cardiac surgery. Integrates exercise, nutrition, stress management, and education to improve heart function and reduce future risk.

Conditions We Treat

Cardiology

Focuses on medical diagnosis, monitoring, and non-invasive management of heart-related symptoms and diseases.

Symptoms & Risk Conditions

  • Chest pain
  • Irregular heartbeat (Arrhythmia)
  • High blood pressure (Hypertension)
  • High cholesterol
  • Palpitations & syncope (fainting spells)
  • Breathlessness (dyspnea)
  • Recurrent chest pain or breathlessness

Medical Cardiac Conditions

  • Coronary Artery Disease (CAD) – early stages
  • Heart failure & Cardiomyopathy
  • Valve disorders (initial diagnosis and follow-up)
  • Bradycardia, Tachycardia (managed with medication or monitoring)
  • Weak heart (low ejection fraction)
  • Inflammatory conditions: - Pericarditis - Endocarditis
  • Heart block (early detection & monitoring)
